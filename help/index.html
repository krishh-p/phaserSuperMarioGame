Purpose Of The Game

You are controlling the Mario character in the game. The goal is to gather as many points as possible and make it to the end without dying. There will be a series of obstacles in your way of doing that. There will be 3 levels for you to complete the game.

Controls

Use ← to move left
Use → to move right
Use ↑ to jump up

How to get points

1. Collect coins that are laying on the surface this will gain you 50 points
2. Hit your head on mystery blocks to get a coin
3. Kill a goomba by stepping on top of it this will gain you 100 points

How to die

• Contact with a goomba (not jumping on top of it) will kill you.
• Falling out of world bounds (underneath the ground) will kill you.
• Any contact with fire in the final level will kill you

Different Obstacles

• Jump on top of the green tubes to pass them. You may need to jump from tube to tube to pass!
• Bricks act as solid players and do not break. This allows players to move arround them and use them as platforms
• Treat the stairs the same way you would treat a brick; it's a solid object that the player can maneuver around
• Mystery blocks are solid blocks that do not break just like the bricks, except they produce a coin when the player's head it hit against the bottom of the block

Ladders

To go up a ladder, make sure your player is overlapping with the ladder and you're holding the UP key at the same time. This will bring you up the ladder. These are added so you can go back in the map, you don't get stuck (have an escape), and to reach higher platforms in the map.
